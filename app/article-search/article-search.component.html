    Microsoft are running a free program called Visual Studio Dev Essentials. It has a range of useful and not so useful 'developer benefits' included in it. Two that I've found valuable are the 3 month free Pluralsight subscription and the free monthly Azure credits. There are a broad range of other resources included though and from the ones I've tried they are totally free-to-try, not "add your credit card and remember to cancel it before we charge you".
